Warning: Due to a bug correction, RetroShare now doesn't keep re-hashing files which name contains a comma. Although the new file list is in general backward compatible, people running a previous version won't be able to read your file lists if they contain filenames with commas. In such a case, suppress your commas, or have your friends update!
